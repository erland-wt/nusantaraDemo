WELCOME TO NUSANTARA X-RAY

Nusantara X-ray is a merchant site under the auspices of PT Pola Inti Integrasi which provides information and sales regarding x-ray products for security, events or other purposes, Nusantara X-ray also provides updated information in our News & Events and Articles for information regarding x-rays, metal detectors and other security systems.

X-ray is a form of electromagnetic radiation with a wavelength ranging from 10 nanometers to 100 picometers (similar to frequencies in the range of 30 PHz to 60 EHz). X-ray is commonly used in the diagnosis of medical images and X-ray Crystallography.

This x-ray scanner can display scanned objects on the monitor screen, including the material or type of material of the object, such as whether the object is non-organic (metal), organic, or synthetic. In addition, x-ray scanners can also detect objects that are explosives or narcotics. When equipped with an explosive detector, it will be a complete combination of security inspection systems for important areas.
